Terrestrial Succession West


Concept

This area of approximately 9 acres lies to the West of the diagonal path separating it from Succession East. It lies along the steep hillside bounded on the lower Northern side by the Locust Shoulder and on the upper Southern side by the Field UpTop, home of the Red Oak – Northern Hardwood planting. It also includes a tongue of land extending to the South-West of the main property. Most of this site is on a very steep North-facing hill that is overgrown with a variety of native hardwood and Pine Trees as well as non-native invasive shrubs. It is nearly inaccessible and, thus, nearly unvisited. We have recently had a narrow logging-type “road” cut into its interior that will allow future access and, perhaps, more ambitious engagement.
