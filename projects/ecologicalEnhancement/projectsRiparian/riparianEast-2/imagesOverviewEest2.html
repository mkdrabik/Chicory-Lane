Riparian East 2:
Images

Early

Images from Fall, 2022, show early stages of the project, before significant site preparation was begun and before significant plantings of native species. This section of stream is unusual within ChicoryLane. It lies at the base of  a steep hillside that is home to a small Hemlock - Red Oak - Mixed Hardwood plant community not found elsewhere on the property. The topography of this area includes an east to west flowing stream, called  Green Grove Run, and associated small  floodplains. At the start of this project, one can note light inclusions of typical invasives - Honeysuckle, Multiflora Rose, and Black Walnut. But the more desirable signature species - Hemlock, Beech trees, Hophornbean, American Hornbean, are well represented. Of especial note are broad, wide coverings of several desirable herbaceous species, a sample of which is included, here. Thus, this area is unusual in the opportunity it provides for enhancement while requiring relatively modest remedial attention to invasives.
