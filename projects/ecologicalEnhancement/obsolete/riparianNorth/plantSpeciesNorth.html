Riparian North

Native Plant Species



Species of native trees, shrubs, and herbaceous plants currently found in the Riparian North project area. By studying the particlar benefits  or ecological services (or liabilities) provided by each, we will consider enhancements that might derive from increasing or decreasong their numbers. We will also identify additional species that might be introduced that would extend these benefits/service in specific ways.
From these considerations, we will work toward a plan for making actural changes to the site, which will, in turn, lead to further observation and assessment.
