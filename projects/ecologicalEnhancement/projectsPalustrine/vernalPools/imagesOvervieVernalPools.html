Palustrine Vernal Pools:
Images

Early

Images from June, 2023, show an intermediate stage of this project. Significant work has been done on the site including planting numerous shrubs surrounding both pools. Circular paths have been cut and are maintained encircling both pools. These paths have been extended to include a natural area west of the pools that both provides views of the stream as well as the Willow Grove at the bottom of Riparian SouthWest. Work is now shifting to develop encircling large canopy trees to provide more shade for the pools. This will be done by extending the palette of trees in the western third of the area to the rest. We have been pleased to see New York Ironweed make its way to this area; we will add more herbaceous species suitable for this mixed environment.
