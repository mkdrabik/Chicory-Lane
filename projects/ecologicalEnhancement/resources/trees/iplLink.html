2023 PA IPL CBF Tree Request
Thank you for your interest in requesting trees through the partnership between Chesapeake Bay Foundation and Pennsylvania Interfaith Power & Light.

Pennsylvania Interfaith Power & Light is a community of congregations, faith-based organizations, and individuals of faith responding to climate change as a moral issue, through advocacy, energy conservation, energy efficiency, and the use of clean, renewable energy. To learn more about our work, visit our website: https://paipl.us/ 

You do not need to be part of a religious community or claim a religious identity to receive trees through this program. Questions on this form that collect this data help us know who we are reaching and how to better serve communities of religion and conscience across PA. 

You are submitting a plant request - for a variety of reasons CBF may not be able to completely fill your request and that may not be clear till mid-August. We then hope to give you a better idea of what you will likely receive and when you can pick them up at your chosen host site.

Trees will be delivered somewhere in-between mid September and late October (dates should be more specific as we get closer to distribution date). Once trees are available at your requested location for pick up, you will be notified. Please note that this is a request form, your request is only confirmed once the entire order has processed and availability has been communicated to you. You are able to request any number of trees from any variety of species. Please be patient as it takes time for our volunteer-led effort to process requests, order trees, and organize distribution logistics.

***All trees are distributed on a first-come-first-serve basis and we cannot guarantee specific species***

If you are ordering for several different sites, with different addresses or nearest intersections, please fill out a separate order form for each, but give your same contact information on each order form. Only change the geographical information on each new form.  

By submitting this form, you agree that your email address will be collected by Pennsylvania Interfaith Power & Light, and you will receive communications about other opportunities to connect with our programs and care for our shared environment.

If you are interested in donating to support this work, please go here: https://give-usa.keela.co/tree-donation
