ChicoryLane Model:
Principles and Assumptions

 

  • Foundational Concepts
    • Improve Ecological Quality of the Land
    • Recognize Land's  Intrinsic Value
    • Recognize Ecological Balance as a Fundamental Virtue
    • Provide sense of Engagement

  • Secondary Concepts
    • Target: landowners of parcels 10 - 100 acres
      • Direct Landowner participatio, at least at the level of management
      • Concepts can be extrapolated and scaled to parcels of other sizes

    • Presumptions
      • Open-Ended Timescale
      • Varying Financial Commitment

    • Encourage Landowners to share Accomplishments and Experience
