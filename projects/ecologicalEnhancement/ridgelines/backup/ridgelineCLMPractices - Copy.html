ChicoryLane Model:
Practices

 

  • Select site(s)
    • Ecologically coherent
    • Comfortable scale (e.g., 1-3 acres)
    • Need or oppotunity

  • Record it
    • Map site (informal)
    • Record images
      • Before, during, and after
    • Identify natural features
      • Stream, rock outcroping, wet area, northern or southern hillsides, etc.)
    • Identify native species
      • Plants, birds, wildlife, etc.

  • Designate working components
    • Palettes of species
      • Desirable
      • Undesirable
      • Complementary introductions
         
  • Consider and plan changes
    • Palettes to increase, decrease, introduce
      • Explain why for each
    • Map intended changes
    • Designate sequences of actions
    • Identify resources needed
      • Inside effort
      • Outside effort
      • Materials
      • Financial
      • Other

  • Implement
    • Timeline
    • Prioritize and Organize
    • Accomodate (or not) the Unexpected
      • It's YOUR project

  • Assess
    • Consider: has your project improved the Ecological Quality of the area?
      • If so, in what ways
      • If not, why not
      • What would you do differently
    • Explain and document
    • Qualitative, rather than Quantitative, emphasis
    • Note relation with surrounding areas
