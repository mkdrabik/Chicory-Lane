ChicoryLane Model:
Principles and Assumptions

 

  • Landowners of parcels 10 - 100 acres
    • Direct Landowner participation 
    • At least at managment and decision-making levels

  • Foundational Concepts
    • Improve Ecological Quality of the Land
      • its ecological health and diversity
    • Recognize Land's  Intrinsic Value
      • Over and above Services provided to Human interests
    • Recognitize Ecological Balance as a Fundamental Virtue
      • As opposed to Unconsidered Ecological Divrsity
        • Diversity for the sake of diversity
        • Sheer number of species)
    • Provide sense of Enagement
      • Accomplishment, Joy, Personal
      • Not Overwhelming, Never-ending, Obligation

  • Presumptions
    • Open-Ended Timescale
      Varying Financial Commitment

  • Encourage Landowners to share Accomplishments and Experience
